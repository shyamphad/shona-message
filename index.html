Pune Se Daman Tak: Ek Dil Ki Yatra

By Our Dil Affairs Correspondent

Ek Marathi mulga, jo Pune ki galiyon mein pala bada hua, aaj Daman ki ek ladki ke liye apna dil haath mein liye ghoom raha hai.

Sources ke mutabik, ladki half-Kokani aur half-Guju hai — ek aisi combination jo, jaise experts kehte hain, "sea food aur fafda dono ka maza ek saath milta hai."

Ladke ka dil subah se raat tak usi ke khayal mein rehta hai. Phone check karna, wait karna — yeh sab uski daily routine ban gayi hai.

Kokani-Guju Mix: "Rare & Precious" — Experts

Pyaar ke maahiron ka kehna hai ki half-Kokani half-Guju ladkiyan "ek alag hi level ki hoti hain."

"Kokani side se samundar jaisi gehraai, Guju side se zindagi ki mithaas — yeh combination toh poori duniya mein rare hai," bole ek anonymous source jo khud Daman ke paas rehta hai.
